Microscopiq

Browser-based microscope capture: live preview, scale calibration, on-screen measurements, and PNG export.

Camera

Use Start camera in the middle of the preview when the stream is off. There is no in-app stop; reload the page to reset the camera.

If the camera does not start, try unplugging the USB device, plugging it back in, reloading the page, and checking site permissions for the camera.

Calibration and scale bar

Calibrate opens a dialog: enter the real length (in millimeters) matching the yellow line, then align that line on the specimen. Use Calibration done when finished, or Cancel calibration to abort.

After calibration, a ~1 mm scale bar appears on the preview. You can drag it to a clearer position. Open Calibrate and use Clear scale in that dialog to remove calibration and measurements.

Measurements

Measurements are only available after calibration. Drag on the image to draw a line; select a line to adjust endpoints. Clear removes all measurement lines; Delete selected removes the current selection.

Capture

Capture saves a PNG of the current frame. Capture with measurements includes measurement overlays and the scale bar when calibrated.

Choose Burst mode for multiple frames with a set count and interval; press Space while capturing to cancel the rest of a burst.

Keyboard shortcuts

  • Enter — Capture
  • Shift + Enter — Capture with measurements
  • Space — Cancel burst (while a burst is running)
